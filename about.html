Our Story

Founded and registered in Delhi, DCM & ASSOCIATES has grown to become a leading Chartered Accountancy firm serving clients across India with a primary focus in Delhi NCR.

With over 14 years of combined experience, our partners CA Akshey Kumar and CA Manish Mittal lead a team of dedicated professionals committed to providing comprehensive financial solutions. We specialize in Virtual CFO services, IPO consulting, transaction advisory, and complete compliance management.

Our client-centric approach and deep industry expertise have helped businesses navigate complex financial landscapes and achieve sustainable growth.
